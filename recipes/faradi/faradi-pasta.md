# Faradi Pasta

A fasting-friendly pasta made with cassava (Jovial brand faradi pasta) in a simple ghee-and-faradi-flour white sauce. No onion, no garlic.

## Ingredients

### Pasta
- Jovial faradi (cassava) pasta — 150 g
- Oil — a small drizzle (for the boiling water)
- Salt — for the boiling water

### Sauce
- Ghee — 2–3 tbsp
- Faradi flour — 3–4 tbsp
- Milk — 400 g
- Salt — 1/2 tsp
- Sugar — 3 tsp
- Black pepper — to taste

## Steps

1. Bring a pot of water to a boil with a drizzle of oil and a pinch of salt. Cook the faradi pasta per box instructions, then drain.
2. In a separate pan, melt 2–3 tbsp ghee on medium heat. Add 3–4 tbsp faradi flour and roast it for a few minutes until it smells nutty and loses the raw flour smell.
3. Pour in 400 g of milk while stirring continuously to avoid lumps. Let it come together into a sauce.
4. Add 1/2 tsp salt, 3 tsp sugar, and black pepper to taste. Stir and let the sauce thicken to your liking.
5. Add the drained pasta to the sauce. Stir well to coat every piece, and serve hot.

## Notes

- Sugar: 2 tsp was on the low side — 3 tsp gives the right balance against the pepper and salt.
- Roast the faradi flour properly before adding milk; otherwise the sauce tastes raw.
- Adjust milk if the sauce gets too thick after sitting — faradi flour continues to thicken off the heat.
